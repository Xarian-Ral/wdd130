Whitewater Wilds Rafting

At Whitewater Wilds, we bring safe, guided river adventures to paddlers of all experience levels. Our certified guides prioritize safety while delivering unforgettable outdoor experiences for families, friends, and corporate groups.

History

Founded in 2003 by a team of river guides, Whitewater Wilds began as a small guiding outfit and has grown into a full-service rafting company offering multi-day expeditions and family-friendly day trips.
