Featured Adventures

Family Adventure Float

Difficulty: Class I-II Duration: 4 hours From $89/person

Perfect for families with children ages 6 and up! This gentle float down the Colorado River combines mild rapids with stunning scenery. Our experienced guides share local wildlife knowledge while ensuring everyone stays safe and has fun. Includes lunch, safety equipment, and professional photos.

  • Ages 6+ welcome
  • Professional guide included
  • Lunch and snacks provided
  • All safety equipment included
  • Photo package available

Intermediate Rapids Challenge

Difficulty: Class III-IV Duration: 6 hours From $145/person

Step up the excitement with our intermediate rapids adventure! Navigate thrilling Class III-IV rapids while enjoying breathtaking canyon views. This trip requires some physical fitness and swimming ability. Perfect for adventure seekers looking for more than a gentle float.

  • Ages 12+ with swimming ability
  • Physical fitness required
  • Expert safety briefing
  • Wetsuit rental available
  • Post-trip celebration meal

Extreme Whitewater Expedition

Difficulty: Class V Duration: Full Day (8 hours) From $225/person

For experienced rafters only! Tackle the most challenging rapids our rivers have to offer. This full-day expedition features Class V rapids, technical maneuvers, and stunning wilderness access. Requires previous whitewater experience and excellent physical condition.

  • Previous rafting experience required
  • Ages 16+ only
  • Excellent physical condition needed
  • Premium safety equipment
  • Multi-course riverside lunch
